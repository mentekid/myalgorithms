### Permutations
Computes all possible permutations of [0:N-1] and prints them. Running for
N > 10 is not pretty, so it should be avoided. Example runtimes for my computer:

N || t
------
1    0.000002s
2    0.000009s
3    0.000019s
4    0.000236s
5    0.000336s
6    0.001887s
7    0.025233s
8    0.121474s
9    1.278463s
10  16.549099s
